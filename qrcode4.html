How it works

  1. Get an item at the cafeteria and generate a CLEAN UP QR in the app. The cashier scans it.
  2. After eating, go to the recycling centre and generate the recycling QR. Staff there scans it when you bin your item.
  3. Your points appear in the app after the recycling QR is scanned.
  4. Go back to the cafeteria and redeem your points for rewards.
